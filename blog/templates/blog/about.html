About

S.P.M School

S.P.M School was set up in 1918 on the donated land.School atmosphere is very pleasant and school environment is very clean and attractive.The school building is located in the centre of the city .S.P.M School is two storey building having classes for Ist to IVth class students.The school follows CBSE board norms.S.P.M. Schol is known for its standard in bringing out best students every year. School has one big library,Principal office,staff room,one computer lab,one common study room,one big sports ground.On one side of the school there is a big garden.School has highly qualified and experienced teachers who teach the students in very effective and creative manner.School has a proper uniform.School has strict norms of study and hygiene.We teach other things too with the study like descipline,manners,behave well,punctuality and many more etiquette. We celebrates all the important days of the year such as Sports Day,Teacher's Day,Parent's Day,Children's Day,Environment Day,School Anniversary,Founder's Day,Republic Day,Independence Day,Gandhi Jayanthi,Onam Celebration,Chrithmas Celebration and New Year Celebration etc in a good manner.
