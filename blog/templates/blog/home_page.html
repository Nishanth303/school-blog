Welcome to S.P.M School

What makes a child gifted and talented may not always be good grades in school, but a different way of looking at the world and learning from the school.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.A school is temple of knowledge where we can learn how to live our social life.Educational institutions are the true seats of learning.The students ,who are really anxious to learn,can develop healthy habits only in the schools.In a school,students are taught how to move in society, how to behave with others and how to progress in life.

S.P.M School is a well reputed school, enjoys the distinction of being the top school in Kerala with excellent board results and well known achievements in the field of athletic and other extra curricular activities. It was started in the year 1918 as a lower primary and now successfully marching towards with the pride of bringing out the best students every year.

Our school has beautiful infrastructure for class room learning and a well equipped staff of qualified teachers playing a major roll in not just teaching but in shaping and moulding the citizens of tomorrow and also make them shine where they are.

S.P.M school strives to provide the best of modern education and a more holistic educational experience to the young minds. Its ambience teamed with a positive outlook unleashes a dynamic and dazzling climate to fulfilltheir creative urges and provide a platform to showcase their talents.

Thank you for visiting us. We hope this website will convey our sense of purpose, our commitment to excellence, and our devotion to the holistic development of each student.
